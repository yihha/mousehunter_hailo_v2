SOUND FILES FOR AUDIO DETERRENT
================================

Place your deterrent sound file here.

Recommended files:
- hawk_screech.wav - Hawk/raptor scream (most effective)
- loud_noise.wav - Any startling loud noise

Requirements:
- Format: WAV (preferred), MP3, or OGG
- Duration: 1-5 seconds
- Volume: Normalize to maximum without clipping

The default configuration expects: hawk_screech.wav

You can download royalty-free sounds from:
- freesound.org
- soundbible.com
- zapsplat.com

Rename your chosen sound to: hawk_screech.wav
Or update config/config.json to point to your file.
